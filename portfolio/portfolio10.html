Insomnia Album

Insomnia is a personal album cover about how my poor lifestyle can apply to our general society’s burnout culture. Lately, our society has been glorifying unhealthy work habits, and correlating a lack of sleeping, eating, and entertainment with hard work and dedication.I chose a simplistic font and cover style, relying more on text than visual images. The color scheme represents a typical night setting, with glowing luminescent letters. The letters in “Insomnia” are scattered and barely connected, depicting a seemingly scatterbrained mindset running on overdrive. Behind is a geometric disarray of connected 3D shapes, representing the synapses of the overworking mind during times when we should be resting.
