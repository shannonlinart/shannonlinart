Euphoria on Ice

As a figure skater for over 10 years, I'm extremely familiar with the feeling of euphoria one experiences as soon as they step into the world of something they love. In this personal piece, I wanted to paint the feeling of adrenaline filling my body as I take the ice, relishing the work I've contributed over the years. It's as if everything becomes magical, shutting out the outside world and creating a personal space for myself alone. I felt that using acrylic paint would be an effective way to utilize my color palette and blend colors to portray reflections on the ice. I used a limited color palette of mostly blues and pinks to illustrate this picturesque and mystical feeling, as well as creating a harsh contrast of lighting from the outside to illuminate the area. I also included perspective to display the full rink from a more personal point of view. At the far end, a window of the outside is visible with a more colorful, realistic palette to display the contrast.
